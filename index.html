How the Formula Works

This calculator estimates the time (in months) it will take for the Net Asset Value (NAV) to grow enough to cover the current premium, based on BTC yield growth.

The Formula:

t = T ×
ln(mNAV multiple) ln(BTC yield multiple)

Breakdown:

  • t: Months until NAV covers the premium (the result).
  • T: Observation period in months (e.g., time of measured BTC yield).
  • mNAV multiple: Market Cap รท NAV (e.g., 2 means 100% premium).
  • BTC yield multiple: Factor by which BTC yield grows NAV over T (e.g., 2x, 3x).

Explanation:

The formula uses natural logarithms to model exponential growth from BTC yield, extrapolating how long it will take for NAV to match the market cap.

Example: With mNAV multiple 1.5, BTC yield multiple 2 over 12 months, it might take ~7 months.
